Our Beginning

How It
All Began

Somewhere between casual conversations and random days, we found ourselves talking and never really stopping. What felt light at first slowly became something we kept coming back to, without really questioning why.

Then came an unplanned ice cream hangout that felt like any other day, just talking and spending time, but somehow it stayed with us. From there, everything just flowed. No big moments, just small ones that slowly added up.

Now, we're stepping into the next chapter of our lives together, and we're so excited to celebrate this special moment with the people we hold closest to our hearts. Your love and support have meant so much to us, and it would truly mean the world to have you with us on our wedding day. As we count down to this moment, we created this website to keep you updated with all the details. Thank you for being part of our story. We can't wait to celebrate with you.

With love,
Francis & Janett
